Overview

The ManiaCDN is a set of mirrors hosted by the community in order to provide a stable cluster of servers. The files are static game contents, such as common signpacks, images on servers (e.g. uaseco) or music (non-copyright!).

Content

Contents of the CDN are managed by validated authors. They can update their files and it will be available within 12 hours (but mostly 6 hours) on the whole network. Copyrighted content is strictly forbidden!

You can apply as a content provider by clicking the Distribute my Content button. You can also read more information about the process of checking and rules.

Distribute my Content

Management

Servers are automaticly being updated with the newest files distributed over the network. We also check the servers , if they are still updating and will directly suspend it, if it's providing old content.

New servers are always welcome! Please click on the button Donate my Server to see how you can donate to the CDN.
